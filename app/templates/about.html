ABOUT US


SEMAPHORE is a techno-Cultural intra-collegiate fest organized & hosted by the Computer Science Department of BMSIT&M.


Offering a plethora of events,the fest has been an integral part of the holistic development of the students of the Computer Science Department since its inception 4 years ago.A platform for both the tech savvy and entertainers to showcase their brilliance.Semaphore has seen incredible levels of interest and participation over the years, not just within the department,but also across the colege.On a superficial level,the theme is inherently contradictory-the concept of both light and darkness manifested simultaneously.On a metaphysical level,however,neon is a commentary on specific socio cultural dimensions such as urban decay,consumerism, and industrial encroachment.
